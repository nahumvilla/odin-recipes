Pancake Recipe

Description

This recipe contains kefir, which is a fermented milk product that is rich in beneficial bacteria. The oats also provide prebiotic fiber, which helps to feed the beneficial bacteria in your gut. Blueberries are a great source of antioxidants, which help to protect your gut bacteria from damage.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup of plain kefir
  • 1/4 cup of oats
  • 1/4 cup of blueberries

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg, buttermilk, and kefir until well combined.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in the oats and blueberries.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, scoop 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the pan.
  5. Cook the pancakes for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, honey, or maple syrup.
